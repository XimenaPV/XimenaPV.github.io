About me

Born and raised in Peru, I moved to the United States at the start of 8th grade. Leaving my home country was a significant event that required immense adaptability and strength, which I developed and, to the have become one of my strongest assets.

My nationality and culture are key features to my identity and it makes me proud to carry them to showcase them during my daily life. My family and friends are the foundation of who I am. Their support is what upholds my ambition to succeed. I strive to honor my heritage by bringing that same spirit of community and perseverance into every team I join.
